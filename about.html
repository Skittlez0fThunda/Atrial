-Disgruntled Bar Patron

Atrial is a hard rock entity forged in the primordial fires of Hell (or Hartsville, South Carolina, both are pretty hot in the summer). Inspired by the likes of Slipknot, Black Label Society, and Asking Alexandria, the band has been performing covers and original music since April 2019 across venues in the southeast. The merry band of hellions consists of Matthew “Chewy” Sanders on vocals, Clayton Chewning on lead guitar, William Sansbury on rhythm guitar, Topher Weekly on bass, and Alex Melton on drums. The band’s latest release “Havoc” is now available on all major music platforms.
