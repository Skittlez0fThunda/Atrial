Atrial is a hard rock band from South Carolina. The band consists of William Sansbury, Clayton Chewning, Christopher Weekly, Matthew (Chewy) Sanders, and Gavin Hafey. Atrial came to be in April of 2018 with their first self titled EP releasing in July 20th later that year. Atrial has its roots in hard rock and metal, taking influences from Black Label Society, Slipknot, Asking Alexandria, Avenged Sevenfold, and many more. Atrial has a lot of new music on the way with their new single "Burn Me Up" being released April 26th, 2019.
